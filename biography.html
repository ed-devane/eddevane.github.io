Biography

Ed Devane is an Irish sound artist and creative technologist whose work spans music, interactive installations, and innovative digital design. His projects explore the intersection of sound, technology, and performance, inviting audiences to experience art in immersive ways.

With over 15 years of experience, Ed has collaborated with various organizations and communities to develop groundbreaking projects that blur the lines between art and engineering. His journey continues as he explores new creative territories.
